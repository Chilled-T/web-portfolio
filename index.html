About Me

I am a passionate and self-taught software developer who has spent the past few years dedicated to mastering the craft of coding. My journey began out of pure curiosity and quickly evolved into a deep passion for building digital solutions that make an impact. Over time, I have completed a professional certificate in Full-Stack Web Development, which strengthened both my front-end and back-end skills, allowing me to bring ideas to life from concept to completion.

Through countless hours of learning and hands-on practice, I’ve built a variety of projects that showcase my creativity, problem-solving ability, and technical growth. Each project reflects my commitment to improving with every line of code I write. I enjoy experimenting with different frameworks, optimizing user experiences, and developing scalable solutions that demonstrate both logic and design thinking. Coding, for me, has become more than just a skill — it’s an ongoing journey of discovery and innovation.

Beyond programming, I find inspiration in different forms of art and culture. As a devoted Arsenal fan, I admire the team’s resilience and strategy, values that I apply in my own development process. My favorite movie is The Matrix, which resonates with me because of its themes of self-discovery and breaking boundaries — much like my path into tech. And when I’m not coding, I’m usually vibing to trapsoul and R&B, music that helps me stay calm, grounded, and creatively inspired.
